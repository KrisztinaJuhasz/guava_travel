About

Latin America has been a popular tourist destination for decades given its holiday friendly climate zones and welcoming population. Generally speaking, Latin America contains the entire area of South America in addition to Mexico, Central America and the islands of the Caribbean, including 20 countries and 14 dependent territories, taking up approximately 13% of Earth's land surface area. The name Latin America was given to the region as its inhabitants are speakers of one of the Romance languages - primarily Spanish, Portuguese and French.
